Wondering Albatross

It is one of the largest, best known, and most studied species of bird in the world, with it possessing the greatest known wingspan of any living bird. This is also one of the most far ranging birds.

  • Scientific Name: Diomedea exulans
  • Life: 55 years
  • Status: Vulnerable
  • Class: Aves

The wandering albatross has the longest wingspan of any living bird, typically ranging from 2.51 to 3.5 m. They also have a salt gland that is situated above the nasal passage and helps desalinate their bodies, due to the high amount of ocean water that they imbibe. They excrete a high saline solution from their nose.
